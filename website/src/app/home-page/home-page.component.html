Welcome to Ocean Harvest Fisheries

This is the place to find the freshest and authentic Sea Food.

Black Tiger Prawns (Penaeus monodon) – "The Ocean’s Royalty"

Low in calories, rich in selenium and iodine, supports metabolism.

Taste: Sweet, rich, and juicy with a firm texture.

Rohu (Labeo rohita) – "The King of Indian Rivers"

High in protein and omega-3, promotes heart health and brain function.

Taste: Mild, slightly sweet, and flaky.

Indian Mackerel (Rastrelliger kanagurta) – "The Omega-3 Powerhouse"

Packed with omega-3, promotes cardiovascular health and reduces inflammation.

Taste: Rich, slightly oily with a meaty texture.

Hilsa (Tenualosa ilisha) – "The Silver Queen of Bengal"

Extremely rich in omega-3 fatty acids, great for heart health.

Taste: Unmatched richness with an oily, buttery texture.
